Create Impact with Intelligence and Precision

GoodCSR Direct is an intuitive Project Management Platform, carefully crafted based on insights from CSR teams and imaplementation organisations. It reduces the probability of miscommunication, keeps projects on track and budgets in check.

Features

Connect-Plan-Execute-Monitor (CPEM)

Connect with implementation partners, plan your tasks, monitor progress and modify any aspect of the programme in response to implementation hurdles – under one roof!

CSR Report in two clicks

Customise data views for creation of exportable, quantitative and qualitative reports to communicate impact and archive progress in just two clicks.

Single login to the world of CSR

Funders and implementation partners using other Samhita GoodCSR features can easily upload their programme onto GoodCSR Direct and into their portfolio without hassle.

The GoodCSR Advantage
