About the Program

Our mission is to spread education among the underprivileged women / girls / children / youth to make them acquainted with the blackboard learning system, make them able to read and write at the same time to make them self employed we have introduced the skill development training session so that they acquire the required skills set in order to earn their living. We will conduct free educational classes and also will conduct skill development programs form the underprivileged women and youth.

Our volunteers will give free tuition 5days a week and Saturday and Sunday will be holidays.
Skill development program will be held also 5days a week with different timings so that one person can get enrolled in both educational program classes and skill development program too.
Will be given certificates from our ngo after successfully completing of the skill development course and will also guide them in placememnts.
